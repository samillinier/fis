# Lowe's Pro Connect - Quick Start Guide

## Login & Sign Up

### First Time - Create Account
1. Go to login page
2. Click **"Sign up"** link
3. Fill out:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Role (type your job title)
   - District (e.g., "District 123")
   - Store Number (e.g., "Store 456")
   - Password (min 6 characters)
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. You'll be logged in automatically

### Returning Users - Login
1. Go to login page
2. Enter your **Email**
3. Enter your **Password**
4. Click **"Sign In"**

---

## Starting a New Conversation

### Step 1: Click "New Conversation"
- On dashboard, click **"New Conversation"** button (top right)

### Step 2: Fill Out Form
The form is pre-filled with your info. Update if needed:

**Required Fields:**
- **Quote/IMS Number** - Enter the reference number
- **Flooring Category** - Select: Carpet, LVP, Tile, Hardwood, Laminate, or Other
- **What they're questioning** - Check all that apply:
  - ☐ Scope
  - ☐ SOW line item
  - ☐ Pricing delta
  - ☐ Competitor comparison

### Step 3: Submit
1. Review information
2. Click **"Start Conversation"**
3. You'll be taken to the chat page

### Step 4: Send Message
1. Type your question in the chat
2. Click **Send** (or press Enter)
3. FIS POD team will respond

---

## Viewing Conversations

### Dashboard
- See all conversations for your District/Store
- Click any conversation to open it
- Use **Search** to find specific conversations
- Use **Filters** (Category, Status) to narrow results

### Chat Page
- View full conversation history
- Send new messages
- Change status using dropdown (top right):
  - Open
  - In Progress
  - Resolved
  - Closed

---

## Profile Settings

### Access
1. Click your name/email (top right)
2. Click **"Profile Settings"**

### Update Info
- Change Name, Role, District, Store Number
- Click **"Change Password"** to update password
- Enter current password + new password
- Click **"Save"**

---

## Important Notes

### Conversation Visibility
- You can only see conversations from users with the **same District AND Store Number**
- This ensures conversations are properly scoped

### Status Management
- **Open** - New conversation
- **In Progress** - Being worked on
- **Resolved** - Issue resolved
- **Closed** - Complete

### Troubleshooting
- Can't log in? Check email/password, clear browser cache
- Can't see conversations? Verify District/Store in Profile Settings
- Messages not sending? Check internet, refresh page
